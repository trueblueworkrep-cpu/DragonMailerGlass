# Theme Comparison - Visual Guide

## Design Philosophy

| Glass Theme | Dark Theme | Light Theme |
|---|---|---|
| Modern and futuristic | Low-glare focus | Clean and bright |
| Transparent surfaces | Deep panels | Light panels |
| Glow and motion | Crisp contrast | Minimal glow |

## Component Visual Comparison

### Metric Card
- Glass: Glow, blur, and layered translucency.
- Dark: High contrast text with cool accents.
- Light: Clean surface with subtle cyan emphasis.

### Progress Bar
- Glass: Flowing gradients and pulsing glow.
- Dark: Solid fills with low-glare panels.
- Light: Clean tracks with crisp fills.

### Notification
- Glass: Soft glow border, translucent panel.
- Dark: Solid panel, high-contrast text.
- Light: Bright panel, minimal accents.

## Color Palettes

### Glass Theme
- Primary Blue: `#3b82f6`
- Accent Orange: `#f97316`
- Teal Accent: `#06b6d4`
- Background: Dark with blur and transparency

### Dark Theme
- Primary Accent: `#22c55e`
- Background: `#0f172a` to `#1e293b`
- Text: `#e2e8f0`
- Borders: `rgba(148, 163, 184, 0.25)`

### Light Theme
- Primary Accent: `#0ea5e9`
- Background: `#ffffff` to `#f0f9ff`
- Text: `#0f172a`
- Borders: `rgba(14, 165, 233, 0.3)`

## Animation Differences
- Glass: Most motion (glow, morph, blur effects).
- Dark: Minimal motion, calm surfaces.
- Light: Minimal motion, clarity-first styling.

## Use Case Scenarios

### Choose Glass if:
- You want a futuristic, animated dashboard.
- You are showcasing a modern product.
- You prefer rich visual motion.

### Choose Dark if:
- You want reduced glare for long sessions.
- You work in dim lighting.
- You prefer focused, high-contrast layouts.

### Choose Light if:
- You work in bright environments.
- You need maximum text clarity.
- You want a clean, minimal look.

## Performance Profile

| Theme | CPU | GPU | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Glass | Medium | Medium | Blur and glow effects |
| Dark | Low | Low | Minimal effects |
| Light | Low | Low | Minimal effects |

## Accessibility
- Glass: High contrast with glow, best in dim rooms.
- Dark: High contrast, low glare.
- Light: High clarity in bright rooms.
